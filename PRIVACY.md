# Privacy Policy for Post-it Parrot

**Last updated: November 28, 2025**

Post-it Parrot ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our Chrome extension.

### 1. Information We Collect
Post-it Parrot is designed to work locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.
*   **Bookmark Data**: All bookmarks, including titles, content snippets, URLs, and images, are stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage.
*   **No Tracking**: We do not use cookies or tracking scripts to monitor your activity.

### 2. How We Use Information
The information collected is used solely to provide the functionality of the extension, specifically to allow you to save, organize, and view your bookmarks.

### 3. Data Security
Since all data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on the security of your device. We do not have access to your data.

### 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
